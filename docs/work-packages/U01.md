## Required interface

- covariance regularization and dyadic regularization parameter;
- positivity and range of the parameter;
- preservation of `IsWeakSimplexCov` and positive definiteness;
- the Gaussian coupling/map law used by the singular orthant proof;
- convergence of the regularized Gaussian vector to the original vector.

Private helpers may remain private unless U04 or U06 has an identified caller. Do not move lower-orthant-specific frontier or probability-limit lemmas into the Gaussian module.

## Allowed imports

The new Gaussian module may import only layers already below `Orthant/Singular.lean`. It must not import `Orthant`, `Rigidity`, `Scratch`, or `Scaffold`.
