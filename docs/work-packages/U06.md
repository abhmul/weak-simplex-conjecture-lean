## Method

Choose positive-definite witnesses for every dyadic regularization. Derive uniform quadratic and coordinate bounds, place the witness `s` vectors in a compact closed ball, extract a convergent subsequence, and pass `a = coordinateMap r s`, compatibility, and the closed value inequality to the limit through finite-dimensional continuity.
