## Mathematical role

WP09 proved the positive-definite lower-orthant theorem under an explicit
`CenteredProductProperty R` parameter. WP17 now supplies that property for every positive-definite
correlation matrix. The unit diagonal needed by WP17 is exactly `hR.1.2`, so this package removes
the temporary theorem parameter without adding any analytic proof obligation.

This closes the unconditional positive-definite orthant stage. Singular covariance is handled once
in WP19. Relevant plan sections are §§1.4, 3.8, 4.1, 6.2, 7, 10, 11.4–11.6, 12 (WP18), 13–14,
17, and 18. The relevant mathematical source section is §9 of
`notes/simplex_optimality_proof.v3.md`.
